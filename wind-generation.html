Wind Generation Industry

Wind power systems need transformer solutions that perform reliably under harsh outdoor conditions. ETT delivers engineered step-up transformers designed to integrate seamlessly with wind turbines, ensuring efficient energy evacuation and dependable grid connectivity.

Supporting Wind Energy the Right Way

Wind turbines generate electricity at low voltage. Our transformers step up this output to the required grid voltage level, making long-distance transmission safer, more efficient, and with minimal energy loss.

Smart Integration & Flexible Installation

ETT wind-compatible transformers are built for multiple installation environments

Inside the wind tower (top or base)
Pad-mounted outdoor setups
Compact substations within wind farms

Designed with high endurance, strong insulation, and minimal maintenance needs, they deliver consistent output even with fluctuating wind speeds and variable load demand.

Where Our Transformers Are Used

Onshore & offshore wind farms
Wind turbine nacelle and tower installations
Compact wind farm substations
Grid integration hubs and evacuation systems
