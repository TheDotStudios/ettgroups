About us

Leading the Future of Reliable and Efficient Transformer Manufacturing

Electro-Tech Transmission Pvt. Ltd. (ETT) is one of Indias fastest-growing transformer manufacturers, delivering reliable, energy-efficient, and custom-engineered power solutions trusted by industries, utilities, government bodies, and infrastructure developers.

Since 2014, we have focused on quality, safety, and performance, backed by BIS, BEE, ISO certifications and the trust of 200+ distributors and 294+ government projects across India.

Trusted by Industries, Utilities, and Infrastructure developers across India.
