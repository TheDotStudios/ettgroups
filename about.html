Foundation stone laid, marking the beginning of our journey in transformer manufacturing. This milestone represented our commitment to excellence and innovation in the electrical industry.

Got BIS & BEE Licenses, establishing our credentials and compliance with national standards for transformer manufacturing. These certifications validated our commitment to quality and energy efficiency.

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 14001:2015 certifications on 4th August 2017. These international quality management and environmental management standards demonstrated our dedication to world-class manufacturing processes and environmental responsibility.

Successfully renewed ISO 14001:2015 certification on 17th July 2020, reaffirming our commitment to environmental management excellence and sustainable manufacturing practices.

Started manufacturing 5 MVA, 8 MVA and 10 MVA transformers, significantly expanding our production capacity and entering the higher power rating segment to serve larger industrial and utility customers.

Started manufacturing CSS (Cast Resin) and Dry Type transformers, diversifying our product portfolio with advanced technology solutions for specialized applications requiring maintenance-free and environmentally friendly transformer solutions.
